Special Events at Mountain View Inn

Mountain View Inn is an authentically restored historic home that provides a backdrop of elegant ambience for your memorable experience. We know how to ensure that your special occasion will be a resounding success.

Celebrations

For over 57 years Mountain View has been the scene of unforgettable occasions--Birthdays, Christenings, Showers, Anniversaries, Wine Tastings, Memorials and other personal celebrations. We would be delighted to create a special event for you.

Retreats

Whether it is a Girlfriend’s Getaway or Rejuvenation Weekend we have the perfect venue for small groups. Cozy up to a fire in the Great Room, indulge yourself with a massage, reiki, or reflexology in your room or take a walk down one of Norfolk’s many nature paths.

Off-site Business Meetings

Team Building and Strategic Planning flourish in Off-site Meetings away from the office. We offer free wireless internet throughout the Inn and our grounds. You can stay connected in a beautiful natural setting that affords time for relaxation and contemplation.

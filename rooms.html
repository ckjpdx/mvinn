Inn Rooms

Situated on over three acres of scenic mountainside, your privacy and comfort are our first priority. Our historic Inn offers large, uniquely decorated guestrooms, each one lovingly and individually restored with antique beds and other delightful period pieces.

Whether you seek a Personal Retreat, a Romantic Getaway, a Memorable Weekend or a unique site for your next Corporate Meeting, we are happy to meet your needs.

A third person can be accommodated in Room 4 (seasonally) and the Guest House for $35 extra per night.

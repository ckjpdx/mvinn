Directions to MV Inn

67 Litchfield Road
(Route 272)

GOOGLE MAPS

From New York or Southern Connecticut take the Connecticut Turnpike (I-95) to Exit 27A (Route 8 North) and proceed north until the end of the highway (50 miles.) Go west on Route 44 for 10 miles to the Norfolk Village Green and then go left onto Route 272 until you reach the Mountain View Inn.

From New York State (Albany Area) follow Route 90 East to the Massachusetts Turnpike. Take the B3 exit, follow signs to West Stockbridge Massachusetts via Rt 102, in West Stockbridge take Rt. 41 south to Rt 7 in Great Barringon. Follow Route 7 to Route 44 East at Canaan CT., and then follow scenic Route 44 East to the Norfolk Town Green and proceed right onto Route 272 to the Mountain View Inn.

From Western Connecticut follow scenic Route 44 East to the Norfolk Town Green and proceed right onto Route 272 to the Mountain View Inn.

From the Boston Area follow Route 90 West (the Massachusetts Turnpike) to exit 2, follow signs for Rt 102 and Stockbridge Massachusetts. Follow Rt 102 to Route 7 in Stockbridge MA. Follow Route 7 to Route 44 East at Canaan CT., and then follow scenic Route 44 East to the Norfolk Town Green and proceed right onto Route 272 to the Mountain View Inn.

And from Rhode Island and Eastern Connecticut, follow Route 44 West to the Norfolk Village Green and proceed left onto Route 272 to the Mountain View Inn.
